How it works

  1. Select a bundle: Devices ship ready for VPlus Lite, VPlus, or VPick+. Scanners and accessories are optional add‑ons.
  2. We provision: MDM enrollment, Wi‑Fi profiles, and app configuration.
  3. We ship: Devices arrive ready for pair‑and‑go with Bluetooth scanners.
  4. Operate: OS & app updates, and RMA support under your selected SLA.
  5. Refresh: Seamless device refresh at 36 months (or retire/return).
