No Voice Server. No Middleware. No Fragile Architecture.

Traditional voice-picking solutions depend on a dedicated voice server or middleware layer that translates WMS tasks into spoken dialogs and interprets confirmations. This introduces a second point of failure, adds licensing costs, requires IT maintenance, and increases latency — especially in multi-site or high-volume environments.

VPick+ eliminates this entire layer with Serverless Voice Picking™. All voice logic and recognition run directly on the Android device, removing the need for any on-premises or cloud-hosted voice servers.

  • No extra servers to buy or maintain
  • No middleware licensing or support contracts
  • No speech-recognition engines to host or patch
  • No single point of failure between WMS and operators
  • Lower total cost of ownership
