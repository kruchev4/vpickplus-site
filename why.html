Why VPick+

Warehouses are moving beyond voice‑only systems. VPick+ is built around multimodal picking — blending voice, screen, and scanning so every step uses the right mode for the moment.

This isn’t “voice with extras.” It’s a flexible workflow model that adapts to noise, pace, task complexity, operator preference, and site conditions.

Why Multimodal Matters

Legacy voice systems are voice‑restricted. VPick+ is voice‑preferred — not voice‑bound.

  • Noisy environments: Switch to scans and screen clarity without losing pace.
  • Dense SKUs: Visual cues reduce errors and prevent mix-ups.
  • Compliance: Scanning ensures item/lot/quantity accuracy.
  • Training: New hires rely on screen support, experts stay in voice.
  • Flexibility: Pick the mode per step or let “Auto‑Flow” guide operators.

SilentScan™ (Scan‑Driven Mute Mode)

Some areas are simply too loud for voice. SilentScan™ lets operators mute voice temporarily and move through steps using:

  • Barcode scans to advance workflow
  • Lightweight on‑screen confirmations
  • Instant resumption of voice when ready

Ideal for docks, outbound lanes, peak-season noise, packout, and cross‑dock areas.

SilentScan™ Benefits

  • Fewer repeats due to noise
  • Consistent accuracy through scans
  • Operators stay in flow regardless of environment
