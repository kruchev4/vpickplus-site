Warehouse Device Leasing Explained

Reduce CAPEX, accelerate rollout, and keep costs predictable with refresh cycles and SLAs.

Leasing in a nutshell

  • OPEX over CAPEX: Smooth, predictable costs
  • Provisioned and ready: MDM enrollment, Wi‑Fi profiles, app configuration
  • Support: Updates, RMAs, and optional advance replacement
  • Refresh: Replace or retire at the 36‑month mark

What’s included vs. add‑ons

  • Included: Android device provisioning, OS/app updates, RMA handling, lifecycle refresh
  • Add‑ons: Bluetooth scanners, headsets, belt slings/holsters, cases, cradles/mounts

Which bundle?

  • VPlus Lite: Lightweight UI (no voice)
  • VPlus: Programmable UI, richer integrations (no voice)
  • VPick+: Voice + screen + scan with Scan‑Driven Mute Mode
