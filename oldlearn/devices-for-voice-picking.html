What Devices Work with Pick‑to‑Voice?

Selecting hardware for voice and multimodal workflows on Android.

Common device types

  • Rugged Android handhelds: Well‑balanced for day‑to‑day picking.
  • Wearables: Wrist‑mounts and ring scanners for high‑velocity tasks.
  • Enterprise smartphones: Lighter, cost‑effective, and flexible.
  • Headsets: Warehouse‑grade audio with noise reduction.
  • Bluetooth scanners: Optional add‑on—lease from us or bring your own.

Considerations

  • Environment (freezer, ambient, dock)
  • Battery life and charge management
  • Ergonomics across shifts
  • MDM enrollment and update windows
