Why Multimodal Works for 3PLs

Why VPick+ Fits

3PL operations depend on reliability across multiple customers and buildings. Traditional voice systems rely on a dedicated voice server or middleware to run dialogs and speech processing [1][2], creating extra cost and additional failure points.

With Serverless Voice Picking™, VPick+ runs voice, multimodal logic, and SilentScan™ directly on the Android device—no server, no middleware, no backend speech engine. This reduces downtime, simplifies deployment, and lowers total cost of ownership.

  • No voice-server hosting or licensing
  • Lower TCO vs. legacy voice systems
  • More reliable — fewer moving parts
  • Multimodal: voice + screen + scan
  • Easy to scale across sites and client accounts
