Why VPick+ Fits

VPick+ provides a modern, adaptable, and reliable platform that helps teams maintain pace and accuracy across shifts and sites. Traditional voice systems depend on a dedicated voice server or middleware to run dialogs and speech processing [1][2], increasing cost and adding failure points.

With Serverless Voice Picking™, VPick+ runs all multimodal logic—voice, screen, scan, and SilentScan™—directly on the Android device. No middleware. No voice server. No backend speech engine.

  • No voice-server hosting or licensing
  • Lower total cost versus legacy voice systems
  • Higher reliability—fewer moving parts
  • Multimodal workflow flexibility (voice + screen + scan)
  • Easy to scale across buildings and client accounts
