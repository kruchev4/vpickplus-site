Built for 3PL Complexity

Switching customers, workflows, and SLAs requires a flexible platform — not a fragile voice-only system. VPick+ adapts per client profile without retraining or reconfiguration dependencies on legacy middleware.

  • Fast onboarding for seasonal labor
  • Voice + screen + scan for high accuracy
  • Client-by-client configuration without engineering overhead
  • Actionable guidance for audits and VAS tasks
  • SilentScan™ for low-noise, high-scan-rate environments

Why VPick+ Fits

3PL operations demand reliability across multiple customers and buildings. Legacy voice systems rely on a dedicated voice server or middleware layer to run dialogs and speech logic [1][2], which increases cost and creates additional failure points.

With Serverless Voice Picking™, VPick+ runs the entire workflow directly on the Android device — no server, no middleware, no speech engine hosting. This reduces downtime, accelerates deployment, and lowers total cost of ownership.

  • No voice-server hosting or licensing
  • Lower TCO vs. legacy voice systems
  • More reliable — fewer moving parts
  • Multimodal: operators use voice, screen & scan together
  • Easy to scale across buildings and client accounts
