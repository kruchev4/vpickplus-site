Why Multimodal Works for 3PLs

  • Client-by-client flexibility: Configure prompts, confirmations, and scans per SLA—without rebuilding the workflow.
  • Faster onboarding: Familiar Android UI plus voice guidance gets new temps productive in hours, not weeks.
  • Accuracy at speed: Voice for navigation, screen for detail, and barcode scans for verification where it counts.
  • SilentScan™: Scan‑driven mute mode reduces aisle chatter during high‑velocity runs—workers see/feel confirmations instead of hearing them.

Why VPick+ Fits

VPick+ is built for real warehouse environments with rugged, Android‑native multimodal workflows. Operators move faster and make fewer errors through a combination of voice, screen, and scan guidance.

With Serverless Voice Picking™, VPick+ removes the dedicated voice server or middleware that traditional solutions require [1][2]. All logic, speech handling, confirmations, and SilentScan™ behavior run directly on the device—improving reliability and reducing cost.

  • Higher reliability with fewer failure points
  • Lower total cost versus legacy voice systems
  • Android‑native flexibility across devices and sites
  • Fast onboarding across shifts and temp labor
  • Multimodal workflow engine adapts to every process
