Why It Works for Manufacturing & Kitting

  • Step certainty: Voice pacing plus on‑screen visuals ensure correct sequence and orientation.
  • Component verification: Barcode scans capture lots/serials at the point of use.
  • Quality gates: Insert mandatory checks or secondary approvals where needed.
  • SilentScan™: Mutes voice during scan‑heavy phases, switching back automatically when scanning slows.
