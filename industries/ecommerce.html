Why It Works for eCommerce

  • Accuracy at speed: Scan checks prevent look‑alike mispicks and keep return rates down.
  • Short SLAs, steady pace: Voice guides movement while on‑screen details handle complex lines or options.
  • Peak‑ready onboarding: Familiar Android UI reduces training time for seasonal teams.
  • SilentScan™: When scan streaks accelerate, voice output mutes automatically so workers stay focused.
