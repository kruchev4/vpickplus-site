Product Overview

VPick+ is a modern, Android‑native voice solution that combines voice guidance, simple screen prompts, and barcode scanning into a single, adaptable workflow—built for real‑world environments and teams.

Operators get a clean, predictable experience. Supervisors get improved accuracy and throughput. IT teams get a flexible platform that works with existing systems and devices.

How VPick+ Works

Voice keeps the operator moving. Screen provides clear visual confirmation when needed. Scanning ensures accuracy for locations, items, and quantities.

This balanced approach gives every site the flexibility to match its environment, people, and processes.

Scan‑Driven Mute Mode

Keep work flowing in loud or chaotic areas by temporarily silencing voice prompts and advancing with scans and on‑screen cues. Resume voice instantly when conditions improve.

  • Progress in high‑noise zones
  • Accurate confirmations without repeats
  • Clear visual cues during peak activity
