Three Modes. One Workflow.

Voice

Hands‑free confirmations, guided steps, and rapid onboarding with spoken prompts—optimized for noisy environments.

Screen

On‑device visuals for exceptions, lot/expiry checks, images, or multi‑SKU clarity—no mode switching overhead.

Scan

High‑confidence validation with barcode scans; reduce mispicks and speed up confirmations at aisle, slot, and item level.

SilentScan™ — Scan‑Driven Mute Mode

When scanning starts, voice output intelligently mutes. Workers stay focused with vibration and visual cues—ideal for dense pick faces and quiet zones.

  • Automatic voice suppression during rapid scan streaks
  • Haptic + UI confirmations in lieu of audio chatter
  • Seamless return to full voice when scanning stops
