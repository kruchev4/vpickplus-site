Serverless Voice Picking™ — No Voice Server. No Middleware.

Traditional voice‑picking systems rely on a dedicated voice server or middleware layer to convert WMS tasks into spoken prompts and interpret confirmations [1]. This architecture adds cost, latency, and a second point of failure.

Even enterprise systems like SAP EWM depend on external voice middleware for recognition and dialog flow — SAP states that the middleware “handles the actual voice interface,” meaning speech processing does not occur on the device [2].

VPick+ eliminates this entire layer. All multimodal logic, speech behavior, confirmations, and SilentScan™ mode run directly on the Android device — no servers to deploy, no middleware to patch, and no backend voice engine to manage.

  • No voice‑server hosting or licensing
  • No middleware integrations or maintenance windows
  • Higher reliability — fewer moving parts
  • Lower TCO compared to legacy voice systems
  • Instant scalability across fleets and locations
  • Faster response — no server round‑trips

Three Modes. One Workflow.

Voice

Hands‑free confirmations, guided steps, and rapid onboarding with spoken prompts—optimized for noisy environments.

Screen

On‑device visuals for exceptions, lot/expiry checks, images, or multi‑SKU clarity—no mode switching overhead.

Scan

High‑confidence validation with barcode scans; reduce mispicks and speed up confirmations at aisle, slot, and item level.

SilentScan™ — Scan‑Driven Mute Mode

When scanning starts, voice output intelligently mutes. Workers stay focused with vibration and visual cues—ideal for dense pick faces and quiet zones.

  • Automatic voice suppression during rapid scan streaks
  • Haptic + UI confirmations in lieu of audio chatter
  • Seamless return to full voice when scanning stops
