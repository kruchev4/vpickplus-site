Serverless Voice Picking™ — No Middleware. No Voice Server.

Traditional voice‑picking systems rely on a dedicated voice server or middleware layer to translate WMS tasks into spoken instructions and interpret picker confirmations — introducing extra cost, infrastructure, and failure points. Industry references show the voice server as a required component in legacy deployments.[1](https://seobuddy.com/blog/how-to-find-a-sitemap/)

VPick+ eliminates this entire layer. All speech, dialog, and multimodal logic run directly on the Android device, with no cloud or on‑prem voice server required. This removes the complexity that systems like SAP EWM still depend on through mandatory voice middleware.[2](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/vpickplus_industries-vpick-activity-7437429112144609280-CvHk)

  • Zero voice‑server licensing or hosting
  • Fewer moving parts, fewer outages
  • Lower total cost of ownership
  • Instant scale to new sites — no backend load
  • Direct WMS → device communication

Three Modes. One Workflow.

Voice

Hands‑free confirmations, guided steps, and rapid onboarding with spoken prompts—optimized for noisy environments.

Screen

On‑device visuals for exceptions, lot/expiry checks, images, or multi‑SKU clarity—no mode switching overhead.

Scan

High‑confidence validation with barcode scans; reduce mispicks and speed up confirmations at aisle, slot, and item level.

SilentScan™ — Scan‑Driven Mute Mode

When scanning starts, voice output intelligently mutes. Workers stay focused with vibration and visual cues—ideal for dense pick faces and quiet zones.

  • Automatic voice suppression during rapid scan streaks
  • Haptic + UI confirmations in lieu of audio chatter
  • Seamless return to full voice when scanning stops
