What Is LiveMap™?

LiveMap™ is a real-time, dynamically updating floor view that shows supervisors exactly where active pickers are working inside the distribution center. It’s powered directly by the VPick+ location field—no beacons, no RTLS hardware, no add‑ons.

/assets/images/livemap-21x9-banner.jpg" alt="LiveMap real-time DC visibility">

Why Real-Time Visibility Matters

In high‑velocity warehouse environments, supervisors need more than historical reports—they need awareness of what’s happening right now. LiveMap™ provides operational clarity to help you:

  • Balance work across zones by seeing where labor is clustered.
  • Reduce congestion in aisles before it slows picking.
  • Monitor progress without walking the floor.
  • Spot idle time or bottlenecks instantly.
  • Increase supervisor reach across large DCs.

With LiveMap™, leaders spend less time searching for people—and more time improving flow.

How LiveMap™ Works

LiveMap™ uses real‑time workflow telemetry from the VPick+ app. When a worker receives a task or confirms a pick, VPick+ records the active aisle and bay as part of the normal picking process. LiveMap™ uses this existing location field to place each picker on the DC floor plan.

There’s no additional hardware required

Because VPick+ runs on Android handhelds and wearables, the system already knows every picker’s task location. LiveMap™ simply brings this data to life visually—turning ordinary pick confirmations into a real-time operational picture.

LiveMap™ updates automatically as each picker moves from slot to slot. Supervisors see pickers gliding across the floor map within seconds.

Use Cases Across Industries

LiveMap™ supports many types of facilities:

  • 3PLs tracking multiple client workflows simultaneously.
  • Retail & grocery distribution where congestion impacts throughput.
  • E‑commerce fulfillment with fast, high‑volume picking lanes.
  • Manufacturing & kitting where visibility improves component flow.

No matter the environment, LiveMap™ lets supervisors understand movement patterns and adjust labor in real time.

How LiveMap™ Is Different From Legacy Systems

Traditional voice systems or RF workflows offer limited visibility. At best, they show last-scan timestamps or task completion logs. LiveMap™ delivers something more powerful:

  • Real-time movement instead of periodic updates.
  • Task-located positions instead of vague “active” states.
  • No proprietary hardware or RTLS system required.
  • Instant visual understanding rather than scanning reports.
