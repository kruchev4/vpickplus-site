What Is LiveMap™?

Real‑time visibility across the distribution center — see exactly where pickers are working as tasks update.

▶ Watch LiveMap™ in Action

Why LiveMap™ Matters

Supervisors need real‑time clarity — not delayed reports. LiveMap™ gives operations leaders a constantly updating picture of where pickers are inside the DC.

  • Balance work across zones based on live movement
  • Reduce congestion before it slows picking
  • See active vs idle time without walking the floor
  • Monitor throughput visually, as it happens
  • Increase supervisor reach in large DC environments

How LiveMap™ Works

LiveMap™ uses the existing VPick+ location field, updated automatically as each picker completes tasks. Every confirmation includes:

  • Aisle location
  • Bay location
  • Timestamp
  • Picker ID

These updates power a real‑time map that shows pickers moving across the floor — without needing Bluetooth beacons, RTLS hardware, or sensors.
