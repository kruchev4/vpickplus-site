What is Scan‑Driven Mute Mode?

A practical way to keep work moving when voice prompts aren’t ideal.

The problem it solves

Noisy areas and high‑traffic zones can force voice repeats or slowdowns. Operators sometimes prefer to move silently and confirm with scans.

How it works in VPick+

  • Operator mutes voice and proceeds with barcode scans + lightweight on‑screen cues.
  • System continues tracking progress and validations.
  • Operator resumes voice instantly when conditions permit.

Where it’s useful

  • Outbound docks and cross‑dock areas
  • Packout or value‑add services zones
  • Any time ambient noise spikes
