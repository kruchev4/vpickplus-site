Why Move Pick‑to‑Voice to Android?

Open hardware ecosystem, predictable management, and lower TCO.

Key reasons

  • Hardware choice: Rugged handhelds, wearables, enterprise smartphones.
  • MDM/EMM: Enroll, configure Wi‑Fi, push updates, and manage at scale.
  • Cost: Avoid proprietary lock‑ins; select devices per site needs.
  • Integrations: Modern API patterns to WMS/ERP and services.
  • Future‑readiness: Flexibility to adapt devices without rewriting your workflows.

VPick+ is Android‑native by design, enabling voice, screen, and scan on the same device—plus Scan‑Driven Mute Mode for loud areas.
