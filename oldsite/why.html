Why Serverless Voice Picking™ Matters

Traditional voice‑picking platforms rely on a separate voice server or middleware layer to turn WMS tasks into spoken dialogs and to interpret operator responses [1]. This legacy architecture introduces latency, increases IT overhead, and creates an additional point of failure in mission‑critical warehouse operations.

Even modern enterprise systems such as SAP EWM confirm that voice middleware handles the actual voice interface, including recognition and dialog flow — meaning the device does not process speech natively [2].

VPick+ takes a different approach. With Serverless Voice Picking™, all speech handling, multimodal logic, confirmations, and SilentScan™ behavior run directly on the Android device. No middleware. No hosted voice engine. No on‑premise or cloud voice server required.

  • Eliminates voice‑server hosting, licensing, and maintenance
  • Reduces downtime and removes a major point of failure
  • Improves responsiveness — no server round‑trips
  • Simplifies deployment across distributed sites and 3PL networks
  • Reduces total cost of ownership vs. legacy voice systems
